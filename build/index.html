Hello,

I am Nathan Langley and I am a designer based in the North of England close to the great city of Manchester.

Having been raised in the rolling hills outside of the London Sprawl, I have been drawn to the Post-Industrial city by its architecture, craggy horizons and industrious spirit.

I call myself a designer but I have worn many hats. I've made websites, created brands and helped shape experiences. Yet all this amounts to taking due course in how a user experiences an object; striving for the simplest and cleanest approach.

Currently I am Design Lead at Evermix, a start up based in the Northern Quarter of Manchester that helps create memorable experiences for dance music afficiandos, clubbers and DJs alike.
